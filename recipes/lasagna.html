ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil (optional)

directions
  1. Cook Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make Meat Sauce: Cook ground beef with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  3. Prepare Cheese Mixture: Mix ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, and a beaten egg.
  4. Layer: In a baking dish:
  5. Sauce → Noodles → Cheese → Repeat
  6. Top Layer: Finish with sauce and lots of cheese.
  7. Bake: 375°F (190°C) for 40–45 minutes.
  8. Rest & Serve: Let sit for 10 minutes before cutting.
