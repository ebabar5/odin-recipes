ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper (optional)
  • Ketchup (optional)

directions
  1. Peel & Cut Potatoes: Slice into thin sticks.
  2. Soak: Place in cold water for 30 mins (removes starch, makes them crispier).
  3. Dry: Pat dry with paper towels.
  4. Heat Oil: In a deep pan or fryer, heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry Once: Cook until soft but not brown (3–4 mins). Remove.
  6. Cool & Fry Again: Let fries rest for 10 mins, then fry again until golden & crispy.
  7. Season: Add salt while hot. Serve.
