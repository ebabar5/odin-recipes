chicken

Home

ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, wings, or breasts)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Buttermilk
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

directions
  1. Marinate Chicken: In buttermilk with salt, pepper, and spices (1–12 hrs).
  2. Prepare Flour Mix: Season flour with paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper.
  3. Coat Chicken: Dredge marinated chicken in flour mixture.
  4. Heat Oil: In a deep fryer or pan, heat oil to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Check Doneness: Inside should be 165°F (75°C).
  6. rain: Let chicken rest on paper towels. Serve hot.
